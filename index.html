IMPORTANT NOTICE

Indalo Credit Repair is an ITC Education, Finance & Credit Specialists.

We are NOT a Debt Counsellor, Debt Review Company, Credit Provider, or Legal Practice.

Our role is to educate, guide, and assist consumers in understanding their credit profiles, improving their creditworthiness, and addressing credit bureau information where applicable.

We do not place consumers under debt review, administer debt review, or provide debt counselling services.

Our services include:

  • Credit education and coaching
  • Credit score improvement strategies
  • ITC profile analysis
  • Credit report guidance
  • Affordability and finance readiness coaching
  • Credit profile rehabilitation support

Our mission is to empower consumers with knowledge and practical solutions to achieve better financial opportunities and access to credit.
